ABOUT

This is an app that can be used to verify a person's OTP.

The app allows for two ways of validating OTP's.

Firstly it allows a person to select the event they wish to have access to, and then enter their OTP they received via Email.

Secondly if the room has an OTP generated( via Admin on request) then the person can simply scan the QR-Code at the room and then enter the OTP to that room.
